Slime Mold Facts

Slime molds are not fungi but are classified as protists. They can move at speeds of up to 1cm per hour!

Welcome to Slime Mold on the Run!

You are a colony of Physarum polycephalum, a fascinating yellow slime mold with extraordinary abilities. Your goal is to travel from Kamloops to Vancouver, BC - a journey of approximately 350 km!

As a slime mold, you:

  • Can find optimal paths through complex environments
  • Need moisture and nutrients to survive
  • Can split your colony to explore multiple paths
  • Are sensitive to light, temperature, and terrain

Make strategic decisions to navigate the diverse terrain of British Columbia. Watch out for dry conditions in the Interior, challenging mountain passes, and human activity along the way!
